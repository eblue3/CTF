2. General Description of the Fifth Annual Flare-On Challenge and its Puzzles/Challenges.  The Fifth Annual Flare-On Challenge consists of twelve (12) binary puzzles.  The puzzles may be in the form of any digital binary:  PDF, Microsoft Program, Android Application, Image file, Document, Malware, etc.  All of the puzzles/challenges were written and are owned by FireEye.  To participate, each Contestant will register and then they can download the first puzzle.  Each challenge/puzzle will focus on different disciplines of reverse engineering, threat intelligence, programming, etc., and the goal is to find a legal way to extract the embedded “key” to move on to the next challenge/puzzle.  Upon successfully completing each puzzle/challenge, the Contestant is able to obtain the “key” that unlocks the next challenge/puzzle.  The Contestant will not be able to move on to the next challenge/puzzle until she/he successfully completes the current challenge/puzzle.  Each challenge/puzzle gets more difficult with potentially multiple challenges as each Contestant strives to reach and solve the final puzzle.  If a Contestant successfully completes all twelve (12) puzzles, that Contestant will win a prize (as indicated below).
